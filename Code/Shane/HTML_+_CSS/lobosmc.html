What We Do

The Lobos MC - A membership sharing the love of motorcycles since 1964.
​​For decades, The Lobos MC, has dedicated itself to the love of motorcycles. Our members are diverse; enjoying dirt bikes to cruisers, trials bikes to dual sports. As such, we host ISDE style races, Poker runs, Cross Country and Dual Sport events every year. Lobo organized events are always family friendly as fostering young rider interest is important to our Club.
Lobo members strive to preserve and protect our riding opportunities, for ourselves and future generations. The trails we clear, the bridges we build, and the legislation we support is paramount to ensuring continued OHV use on public and State land. While many members work to rectify trail erosion issues within our National Forests, others volunteer their time to the Oregon Motorcycle Riders Association
( OMRA ), working on legislative issues that affect off-road riding.
Being a Lobo’s member requires a love of motorcycles; a drive to promote the sport; and a need to protect the trails on which we ride. By this, we honor those gone before us and hope the future generations thank us. We are the Lobos MC.
